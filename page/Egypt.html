Istanbul: The Jewel Between Europe and Asia


Istanbul is a unique city situated at the crossroads of two continents:
Europe and Asia. It's a place where age-old traditions blend with
modernity, and every corner of the city breathes history.


Attractions:


Hagia Sophia - A majestic cathedral built in the 6th century, which
served Orthodox Christians, later became a mosque, and today
stands as a museum.-

Blue Mosque - Another architectural gem, known for its
magnificent blue ceramic tiles.

Topkapi Palace - The palace of the Ottoman sultans, where one can
view unique exhibits, including sacred relics of Islam.
